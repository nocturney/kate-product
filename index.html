ABOUT

My name is Kate and I have always been passionate about design. My primary role is to create visually stunning and functional websites. My goal is to ensure that the website is easy to use and navigate, while also communicating the message and purpose of the site effectively.

I am proficient in Figma, Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. These tools enable me to create visually appealing and functional websites with precision and creativity.

I started my design path back in 2019, when I got enrolled in Brigham Young University Idaho. Since then, I've done plenty projects both commercial and just as a practice.

FAQ

Can you walk me through your design process?

My design process includes competitor and target audience analysis, designing the logical structure, prototype development, moodboard creation and references, design development, animations, adaptive design, making revisions, and a project presentation with ongoing process monitoring. This structured approach ensures the best results for your project.

Can you help with SEO?

While I primarily focus on design and development, I can incorporate basic SEO best practices into your website design. For more advanced SEO services, I recommend working with an SEO specialist.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes, I can help redesign and update your existing website to improve its look, functionality, and user experience.

Do you provide website maintenance and support?

Yes, I offer 10 days of support after the project handover to help with any questions or issues that may arise.
